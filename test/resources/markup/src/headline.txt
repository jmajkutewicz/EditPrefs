== Ayumi Hamasaki ==
===2016–2017: ''Made in Japan''===
Hamasaki confirmed that she had been working on her seventeenth studio album for 2016 through a post published on Instagram.<ref>{{cite web|author=Hamasaki, Ayumi|title=Heard that today n tmrw will b snowy days so I changed my plans and just started working on my new album at home |url=https://www.instagram.com/p/BA4eurHJPlt/ |archive-url=https://ghostarchive.org/iarchive/s/instagram/BA4eurHJPlt |archive-date=December 26, 2021 |url-access=registration|website=Instagram|access-date=January 27, 2016}}{{cbignore}}</ref> A 15th anniversary edition of Hamasaki's 2001 [[greatest hits album]] ''[[A Best]]'' was released on March 28, 2016.<ref>{{cite web|title=『A(ロゴ) BEST -15th Anniversary Edition-』|url=http://avex.jp/ayu/news/detail.php?id=1031568|website=Ayumi Hamasaki official website|access-date=22 February 2016}}</ref> In May 2016, Hamasaki started her nationwide tour ''Arena Tour 2016 A: Made in Japan'', which was expanded version of her 2015-2016 Countdown Live ''Made in Tokyo''.<ref>{{cite web|title=『"ayumi hamasaki ARENA TOUR 2016 A(ロゴ) ～MADE IN JAPAN～"』開催大決定！！|url=http://avex.jp/ayu/news/detail.php?id=1031561|website=Ayumi Hamasaki official website|access-date=21 February 2016}}</ref> On May 11, Hamasaki made a surprise release of her 17th studio album, entitled ''[[Made in Japan (Ayumi Hamasaki album)|Made in Japan]]'', through music streaming website AWA, where it reached 1 million legal streams after 5 days of release.<ref>{{cite web|title=BIG NEWS!! 「AWA」にて、6月29日発売のニューアルバム「M(A)DE IN JAPAN」が、CD発売の1ヶ月半前の5月11日(水)より全曲独占先行配信決定!!|url=http://avex.jp/ayu/news/detail.php?id=1035694|website=Ayumi Hamasaki official website|access-date=15 May 2016|language=ja|date=11 May 2016}}</ref><ref>{{cite web|script-title=ja:浜崎あゆみ再生100万回、新アルバム先行配信|url=http://www.nikkansports.com/entertainment/news/1653144.html|website=Nikkan Sports|access-date=28 May 2016|language=ja}}</ref> The album was released on physical formats on June 29, 2016. With the album peaking at number two on the weekly [[Oricon]] chart, Hamasaki became the solo-artist with the most top-ten albums (50).<ref>{{Cite web|url=http://music-book.jp/music/news/news/120810|title=浜崎あゆみ、50作目のアルバムTOP10入り達成＆アルバムTOP10獲得作品数記録更新 - music.jpニュース|website=music.jp|access-date=December 7, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170510113726/http://music-book.jp/music/news/news/120810|archive-date=May 10, 2017|url-status=dead}}</ref>
