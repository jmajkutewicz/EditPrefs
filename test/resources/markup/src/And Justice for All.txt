'''''...And Justice for All''''' is the fourth studio album by American [[Heavy metal music|heavy metal]] band [[Metallica]], released on August 25, 1988, by [[Elektra Records]].<ref name="RIAA"/> It was the group's first full studio album to feature bassist [[Jason Newsted]], following the death of [[Cliff Burton]] in 1986. Newsted had previously appeared on the Metallica [[Extended play|E.P.]] ''[[The $5.98 E.P.: Garage Days Re-Revisited]]'', which was his first recorded work with Metallica. ''...And Justice for All'' was certified [[RIAA certification|8x platinum]] by the [[Recording Industry Association of America]] (RIAA) on June 9, 2003, having shipped eight million copies in the United States.<ref name="RIAA">{{cite web|url=http://www.riaa.com/goldandplatinumdata.php?artist=%22And+Justice+For+All%22|title=American album certifications – ...And Justice for All|publisher=[[Recording Industry Association of America]] (RIAA)|accessdate=May 10, 2013}}</ref>
